Why Does the App Experience Glitches?

The SwaRail Super App handles millions of concurrent users booking tickets, tracking trains, and making payments simultaneously. During peak hours (like Tatkal booking at 10:00 AM or 11:00 AM), the Indian Railways CRIS servers experience massive traffic loads. This can occasionally lead to timeouts, OTP delays, or app crashes on user devices.

Below, we have compiled the most common issues reported by users and how to fix them quickly.

Generic Fix Checklist

If you are facing an unlisted bug or a persistent server error message, run through this quick checklist:

  • Check your Internet: Switch between Wi-Fi and Mobile Data to rule out ISP routing issues to CRIS servers.
  • Storage Space: SwaRail requires at least 50MB of free space to run smoothly without freezing.
  • Android Version: The app is optimized for Android 5.0 and above. Very old devices might face compatibility issues.
  • Disable VPNs: The Indian Railways database actively blocks traffic from foreign IPs and commercial VPN servers to prevent bot booking.

Still Need Help?

If you have tried all the steps above and the SwaRail app is still not functioning, the backend servers might be temporarily down for scheduled maintenance (often happening between 11:45 PM and 12:20 AM IST).
