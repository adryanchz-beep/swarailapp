What is SwaRail?

SwaRail (also known as RailOne) is a revolutionary SuperApp launched by Indian Railways that consolidates ALL railway services into one powerful platform. No more juggling multiple apps - everything you need for train travel is now in one place.

Official SuperApp by Indian Railways & IRCTC
Developed by CRIS - the technology experts of Indian Railways
Replaces multiple apps: IRCTC Rail Connect, UTS, and others
Currently in beta but fully functional
Available for both Android and iOS
