Understanding the SwaRail (RailOne) Ecosystem

How the superapp is revolutionizing the Indian commute through technical integration.

The Unified Interface (UI/UX)

The SwaRail APK is designed to solve "app fatigue." Instead of downloading the UTS app for local trains, the IRCTC app for long-distance, and Rail Madad for complaints, SwaRail provides a single entry point. This integration utilizes a Single Sign-On (SSO) mechanism.

UTS Mobile Ticketing & Geo-fencing

The SwaRail app incorporates a robust geo-fencing algorithm for unreserved tickets. To prevent ticket-less travel misuse, the app allows "Paperless" UTS booking only when you are within a 5km radius of the station.

Real-time Data via NTES

Unlike third-party tracking apps that rely on crowdsourced data, SwaRail fetches data directly from the National Train Enquiry System (NTES), helping you plan your journey down to the minute.
